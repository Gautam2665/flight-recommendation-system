OUR TECHNOLOGY

How We Find Your Perfect Flight

SkyScout uses advanced algorithms to analyze thousands of flight options and deliver personalized recommendations tailored to your needs.

Dual-Algorithm Approach

We employ two distinct algorithms - one optimized for finding the absolute best flights, and another focused on finding the cheapest options.

Comprehensive Data Analysis

Our system processes millions of data points including prices, schedules, airline ratings, and user preferences.

Machine Learning

Our algorithms continuously learn and improve from user interactions and flight pattern changes.

Our Recommendation Algorithms

Best Flight Algorithm

Finds optimal flights considering convenience, comfort, and overall value beyond just price.

  • Optimal travel times
  • Best airline ratings
  • Convenient connections
  • Quality-focused

Cheapest Flight Algorithm

Specialized in finding the absolute lowest fares while maintaining acceptable quality.

  • Lowest possible prices
  • Budget airline options
  • Alternative routes
  • Price-focused

Personalized Experience

Tailored recommendations based on your unique travel preferences and history.

  • Learns your preferences
  • Adapts to your budget
  • Suggests ideal times
  • Improves with each search
